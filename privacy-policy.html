Privacy Policy

Seven Feet Travel collects information you choose to share through this website, including your name, contact details, travel preferences, trip dates, destination interests, budget, and other details related to your travel inquiry.

This information is used to respond to your inquiry, plan travel, make recommendations, and provide travel advisory services.

Form submissions may be processed through third-party form services (for example Web3Forms) to deliver your inquiry by email. Your information is not sold.

If travel planning moves forward, some information may be shared with hotels, cruise lines, tour operators, destination partners, or other travel suppliers as needed to request quotes, confirm availability, make bookings, or support your trip.

To request that your information be updated or deleted, contact Seven Feet Travel by email at paul.totah@fora.travel.
